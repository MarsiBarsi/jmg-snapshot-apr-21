What is Jamigo?

Jamigo is a music game. It helps you make practicing fun and easy. Select a song from the catalogue and play it with a MIDI keyboard or sing.

What do I need?

You only need microphone to sing. You can even use your smartphone. To play MIDI keyboard your browser has to support Web MIDI API.
